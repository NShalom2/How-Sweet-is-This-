Welcome to the colorful world of Skittles! These bite-sized, fruit-flavored candies have been delighting taste buds for generations. Bursting with vibrant colors and bold flavors, Skittles are the perfect treat for anyone who craves a little sweetness and a lot of fun.

Flavours

Skittles had 5 original flavours: Green Apple, Orange, Strawberry, Lemon, and Grape. Part of the fun of Skittles is mixing and matching flavors to create your taste combinations. Whether you prefer to enjoy them one at a time or toss a handful into your mouth for an explosion of flavor, Skittles provide endless opportunities for flavor experimentation.

Experience the diverse flavors of Skittles, from sweet to sour and many others, as you savor the delightful taste that's uniquely your own.

More About Skittles Flavors

Skittles come in a variety of exciting flavors beyond the classic ones. You can explore limited edition flavors, seasonal releases, and even regional exclusives. Some popular additional flavors include tropical fruit, sour, and wild berry. Skittles continually surprise and delight with their flavor innovations.

Fun Facts

  • Skittles were first introduced in the United States in 1979.
  • There are five original fruit flavors of Skittles: strawberry, orange, green apple, grape, and lemon.
  • Skittles are made by Mars, Incorporated, the same company that makes M&M's.
  • The iconic "Taste the Rainbow" slogan was introduced in 1994.
  • Skittles have been used in creative ways, including as edible decorations on cakes and cupcakes.
  • Skittles are the second most popular candy in America
